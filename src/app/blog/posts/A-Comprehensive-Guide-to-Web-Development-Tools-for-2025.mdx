- Improved edge rendering
- Turbopack for blazing-fast builds
- File-based routing with advanced layouts

SvelteKit is loved for its simplicity and compile-time optimization. It reduces runtime bloat and is ideal for teams that want to ship fast.

> **Why it‚Äôs great**: Reactive by default, small bundle sizes, SEO-friendly
